Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a new game?

Tap the menu icon at the top of the screen and select New Game.

How do I undo a move?

Tap the undo button in the top bar to take back your last move.

My statistics seem wrong

Statistics are stored locally on your device. If you reinstalled the app, previous stats will have been reset.

How do I reset my statistics?

Go to Settings and tap Reset Statistics.
