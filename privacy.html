Privacy Policy

Solitaire by Nyangi — Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information Solitaire collects (and what it does not).

Information We Collect

Solitaire does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app has no account system, no sign-in, and makes no network requests.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game statistics (wins, losses, best times)
  • Your preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

Solitaire does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at hello@nyangi.app.
