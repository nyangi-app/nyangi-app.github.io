2. What Data We Collect and Why

2.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Cat Solitaire stores the following data exclusively on your device using your operating system's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Game settings (draw count, sound preferences, display preferences)
  • Game statistics (games played, wins, streaks, best times)
  • Achievement and player level progress
  • Undo treat balance

You can delete all locally stored data at any time via Settings → Reset Progress, or by uninstalling the app.

2.2 Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect the following data to serve ads:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
  • IP address (approximate location derived from it)
  • Device model, operating system version, and language
  • App interaction data (e.g., ad impressions and clicks)

This data is processed by Google LLC. For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we obtain your consent before any advertising data is collected, through a consent dialog powered by Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP). You may withdraw or update your consent at any time via Settings → Privacy Settings.

On iOS 14.5 and later, Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework will ask your permission before your device's advertising identifier is used for tracking across apps and websites. You can change this permission at any time in your iOS Settings.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses data from apps using AdMob.

Legal basis (GDPR): Art. 6(1)(a) — consent.

2.3 Analytics (Firebase Analytics)

The app uses Firebase Analytics (provided by Google LLC) to understand how players interact with the game. Firebase Analytics collects:

  • App usage events (e.g., game started, game won)
  • Device model, operating system version, and language
  • Session duration and app version

This data is pseudonymous and aggregated — it is not linked to your name or contact details. Data is retained for 14 months by default per Google's policy.

For more information, see Firebase Privacy and Security.

Legal basis (GDPR): Art. 6(1)(f) — legitimate interest in understanding and improving the game experience.

2.4 In-App Purchases

If you make an in-app purchase, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not receive or store your payment information. We receive only a confirmation that a purchase was made, in order to unlock the purchased content on your device.

Apple's and Google's own privacy policies govern the processing of your payment data.

Legal basis (GDPR): Art. 6(1)(b) — performance of a contract.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with the following third-party service providers solely to operate the app:

  • Google LLC — AdMob (advertising), Firebase Analytics (analytics). Google acts as a data processor under a Data Processing Agreement. Google may be located outside the EEA; transfers are governed by Standard Contractual Clauses.
  • Apple Inc. / Google LLC — In-app purchase processing (as independent data controllers).

4. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the EEA, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data held about you
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction — request that processing be limited
  • Right to data portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw any consent given at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at nyangi.info@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority — in Germany, the relevant authority is the Bundesbeauftragte für den Datenschutz und die Informationsfreiheit (BfDI).

5. Children

This app is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
